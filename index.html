About Us

Who We Are

Dunamisoe Energies Enterprise is a reputable Nigerian business specializing in the supply and distribution of cooking gas, gas cylinders, gas accessories, kitchen utensils, and household provisions. Since 2015, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and exceptional customer service across Nigeria.

We also engage in the importation and exportation of gas accessories, kitchen utensils, and household products — making us a key player in the regional supply chain.

Our Mission

Deliver safe, reliable, and affordable energy solutions to every Nigerian home and business.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted household energy and provisions supplier.

Safety First

All our gas products meet regulatory standards for safety and quality.
